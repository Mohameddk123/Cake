Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before ordering

✦

Simply click any Order Now button or the Discover More button on your favourite package, fill in your name and phone number, and your order will be sent directly to us on WhatsApp. We'll get back to you within a few hours to confirm all the details.

We recommend placing your order at least 3–5 days in advance for standard packages, and 7–10 days for custom or large occasion packages. For urgent orders, feel free to message us on WhatsApp and we'll do our best!

Absolutely! Every order at House of Sweets is fully customizable. You can choose your flavors, colors, themes, sizes, and personal message. Just let us know your vision in the order notes or over WhatsApp — we love bringing ideas to life.

Yes! We offer delivery within our service area. Please mention your delivery address when placing your order on WhatsApp and we'll confirm availability and delivery fees. Pickup is also available.

We accept cash on delivery and various bank transfer / mobile payment options. Payment details will be shared with you over WhatsApp when confirming your order.

We take allergen requests very seriously. Please inform us of any allergies (nuts, gluten, dairy, etc.) when ordering and we will do our best to accommodate you. Note that our kitchen handles common allergens, so please contact us directly for full ingredient details.
